About Me

Chelsea McGovern is a rare original Austin Unicorn. Though she was born in the southern city of the weird, she has the heart of a wanderer. After she escaped the infamous prison known as "highschool," she put on her snow shoes and traveled Northbound. After searching long and far, she found a settlement in New York where she went to SUNY Binghamton.

There she earned two libral arts degrees of English Rhetoric and Behavioral Psychology and with some personal ingenuity, blood, sweat, and tears she became a professional Social Media Ninja, Blog afficionato, and CMS tamer.

When shes not up to her elbows in code or running around local events, she can be found in a cafe reading about strange medical oddities and collecting hedgehog gifs on her Pinterest.

If you come across a Chelsea roaming in the wild, try to tame it with Sushi, Thai Food and musical comedy. Yet beware you just might end up in her novel.
